Editorial Policy

Effective: March 2026

Our Mission

EnergyPricesToday.com is committed to delivering accurate, timely, and impartial coverage of global energy markets. Our editorial team operates independently from any commercial, political, or industry influence. We do not publish paid content, sponsored articles, or advertorials without clear disclosure.

Sourcing Standards

Our reporting is grounded in primary and authoritative sources. We prioritize data and statements from government agencies (EIA, IEA, OPEC Secretariat), regulatory bodies (FERC, NRC, EPA), recognized industry data providers (Baker Hughes, Platts, Argus), company filings and official press releases, and named expert sources with direct knowledge of the subject matter.

We do not rely on anonymous sources unless the information is of significant public interest and has been verified through at least one additional independent source. When we use estimates or forecasts, we attribute them clearly and note the methodology where available.

Market Data

Commodity prices, rig counts, and other market data published on this site are sourced from government databases, exchange data feeds, and recognized industry benchmarks. We clearly label all data with its source, timestamp, and any applicable delays. Market data is provided for informational purposes only and should not be used as the sole basis for trading or investment decisions.

Editorial Independence

Our editorial decisions are made solely by our editorial team. We do not accept payment or other consideration in exchange for coverage. Advertising, if present, is clearly separated from editorial content. Our analysts and reporters are prohibited from trading energy commodities or securities of companies they cover.

Accuracy and Verification

All facts, figures, and claims in our reporting are verified before publication. When we make errors, we correct them promptly and transparently. See our Corrections Policy for details on how we handle mistakes.

Opinion and Analysis

When our writers offer analysis, interpretation, or commentary, we label it clearly as such. Opinion content is distinguished from factual reporting through labeling and presentation. Our analysis aims to inform, not advocate for any particular market position or policy outcome.

Our Team

Our editorial team includes experienced energy journalists and market analysts with backgrounds spanning oil and gas, renewables, geopolitics, and financial markets. For more information about our contributors, visit our About page.
