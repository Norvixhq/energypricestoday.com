April 12, 2026 — Global Oil Tanker Rates Spike on Red Sea Rerouting Congestion.

Tanker Market Surge

Global oil tanker rates have spiked dramatically as Red Sea rerouting and Hormuz disruptions create unprecedented demand for shipping capacity. VLCC rates on benchmark routes have surged to over $80,000/day — triple the five-year average. The longer transit times around the Cape of Good Hope effectively remove vessel capacity from the global fleet, tightening the market further.
