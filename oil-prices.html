About Oil Price Data

Prices shown represent the most recent settlement, spot, or official selling prices for each benchmark. Data may be delayed — timestamps indicate the last update for each section. International prices are typically reported with 1-4 day delays depending on the source. For real-time trading data, consult your broker or a licensed market data provider.
