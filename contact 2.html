Reporting a Factual Error

If you believe an article, price, or data point on EnergyPricesToday.com contains a factual error, please tell us. Corrections are a core part of how we operate, and we publish updates openly. The clearer your message, the faster we can verify and act.

When submitting a correction, please include:

  • The URL of the article or page
  • The specific claim you believe is incorrect (quote or paraphrase)
  • What the correct information is, in your understanding
  • A source or reference supporting the correction (if available)
  • Your name and how you want to be credited (if at all)

Corrections to data points (prices, production figures, rig counts) are typically verified within 24 hours. Corrections requiring editorial judgment (context, framing, attribution) may take longer. All substantive corrections are noted in the article per our Corrections Policy.

What May Not Receive a Response

We read every message but cannot always reply. Messages that generally do not receive individual responses: unsolicited pitches for guest posts or paid placements, SEO backlink requests, generic marketing outreach, requests for personal market predictions or investment advice, and messages without clear questions or requests. If you\u2019re unsure whether your message fits, send it anyway \u2014 we\u2019d rather hear from you than miss something important.
