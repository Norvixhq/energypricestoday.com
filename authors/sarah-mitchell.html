Sarah Mitchell

Senior Energy Analyst

Sarah Mitchell covers crude oil markets, OPEC policy, and global supply dynamics for EnergyPricesToday.com. With over a decade of experience in energy journalism, she has reported from OPEC summits, IEA briefings, and major industry conferences. Her analysis focuses on how production decisions, geopolitical shifts, and macroeconomic trends intersect to move oil prices.
