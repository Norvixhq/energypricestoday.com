James Carter

U.S. Markets Correspondent

James Carter reports on U.S. oil and gas production, shale economics, and refining margins for EnergyPricesToday.com. Based in Houston, he tracks Permian Basin activity, Gulf Coast refinery operations, and the evolving dynamics of American energy exports. His reporting combines field-level operational knowledge with financial market analysis.
