Corrections Policy

Effective: March 2026

Our Commitment

EnergyPricesToday.com is committed to accuracy in all of our reporting and data presentation. When we make errors, we correct them as quickly as possible and with full transparency. We believe that prompt, honest corrections strengthen reader trust.

Types of Corrections

Factual corrections: When an article contains a factual error — such as an incorrect price, date, name, production figure, or misattributed statement — we correct the error in the article text and add a correction notice at the bottom of the article noting what was changed and when.

Clarifications: When published content is technically accurate but potentially misleading due to missing context or ambiguous phrasing, we update the text and add a clarification notice.

Updates: When new information emerges that materially changes the substance of a story, we update the article and note the update with a timestamp. Updates are not corrections — they reflect evolving news, not errors.

How to Report an Error

If you believe we have published inaccurate information, we encourage you to contact us. Please include the specific article URL, the information you believe is incorrect, and any supporting evidence or sources. Send corrections to: corrections@energypricestoday.com

You can also reach us through our Contact page.

Response Time

We aim to acknowledge correction requests within 24 hours and resolve them within 48 hours. Corrections to market data or pricing errors are prioritized and addressed as quickly as possible given the time-sensitive nature of financial information.

Transparency

We do not silently edit published articles. All substantive changes to published content are noted with correction, clarification, or update notices that include the date and nature of the change.
